# Domain Knowledge Reference

## U.S. Fiscal Year
- Before 1977: July 1 to June 30 (FY1953 = Jul 1952 - Jun 1953)
- After 1977: October 1 to September 30 (FY2000 = Oct 1999 - Sep 2000)
- Transition quarter: Jul-Sep 1976 (between old and new fiscal year systems)

## Key Financial Terms
- **T-bills**: Treasury Bills, short-term (4-52 weeks), sold at discount
- **T-notes**: Treasury Notes, 2-10 year maturity, pay semi-annual coupon
- **T-bonds**: Treasury Bonds, 20-30 year maturity
- **Discount rate**: The difference between face value and purchase price, annualized
- **Coupon rate**: Annual interest payment as percentage of face value
- **Gross federal debt**: Total outstanding debt (public + intragovernmental)
- **Debt held by public**: Gross debt minus intragovernmental holdings
- **Budget surplus/deficit**: Receipts minus outlays
- **ESF**: Exchange Stabilization Fund, managed by Treasury
